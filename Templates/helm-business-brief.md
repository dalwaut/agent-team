# HELM Business Handoff Document
### Handsfree Enterprise Launch Machine — Your Operating Contract with the AI Running Your Business

---

> **How to use this document**
>
> This document is the single source of truth HELM uses to operate your business. Fill it out completely and honestly — the more detail you provide, the more accurately HELM can represent you, make decisions on your behalf, and avoid costly mistakes.
>
> Once submitted, HELM will parse this document to configure itself: your brand voice, your offers, your audience, your rules of engagement, and the limits of its autonomy. Treat it like an employee onboarding packet crossed with a legal brief — thorough, specific, and binding.
>
> **Tips before you start:**
> - Use real numbers where asked. Vague answers produce vague results.
> - For credentials, use the placeholder format shown — a secure credentials handoff process follows separately via HELM's encrypted vault.
> - Sections marked `(required)` block AI activation if left blank. Everything else is strongly recommended.
> - You can update this document at any time. Re-uploading it triggers a full re-configuration.
> - The `CRED::` prefix on any field marks it as a secret — HELM routes these directly to its encrypted vault and never logs them.
