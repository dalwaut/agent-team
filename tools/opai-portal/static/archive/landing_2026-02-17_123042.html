FAQ
Common questions about OPAI.
What is OPAI?
OPAI is a private AI operations server. It combines 25 specialized AI agents, a cloud IDE, project management, automation tools, and a secure file system into one platform. Think of it as your AI engineering team — always on, always coordinated, conducted by Mozart.
What makes OPAI different from ChatGPT or other AI tools?
Most AI tools give you a single chatbot. OPAI gives you 25 specialist agents that work together in coordinated squads — like an orchestra, not a soloist. They audit code, plan features, write tests, monitor emails, and manage releases simultaneously. Plus you get a full cloud workspace with IDE, file manager, and project management built in.
How do I access OPAI after purchase?
After payment, you'll receive a welcome email with your login credentials and setup instructions. Access is through your browser at your dedicated OPAI URL. No software to install.
Is my data private?
Yes. OPAI runs on dedicated infrastructure. Your code, conversations, and files are never shared with other users or used for AI training. You have full admin control over your workspace.
What does "founding member pricing" mean?
Beta members lock in their subscription rate permanently. When prices increase at general launch, your rate stays the same for as long as your subscription is active. It's our way of thanking early adopters who help shape the platform.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, all plans are month-to-month with no long-term commitment. Cancel anytime from your account settings or contact us directly. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.
What AI models does OPAI use?
OPAI uses Claude by Anthropic as the primary AI model. The 25 agent roles are specialized through custom prompts and configurations — not different models, but different expertise profiles applied to the same powerful foundation.
