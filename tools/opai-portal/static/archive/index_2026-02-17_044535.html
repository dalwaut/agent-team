Mozart — The AI Orchestra

Not pipelines. Not chains. Performances.

OPAI reimagines AI orchestration through the language of music. Every task is a score. You are the composer. Mozart — the orchestrator — reads your intent, assembles the right ensemble of specialist agents, and conducts them through coordinated execution. Each agent plays its part, in harmony, on tempo. The result is a performance — delivered as actionable reports, completed code, or decisions ready for your review.

17 Squads

Pre-composed ensembles for common workflows — audit, ship, review, plan, evolve, and more. From a string quartet to a full symphony.

💡

Resource-Aware

Mozart monitors CPU, memory, and disk in real time. When the system is under load, it defers non-critical work automatically — no dropped notes.

👥

Human-in-the-Loop

The composer always has final say. Non-trivial decisions surface to you for approval before execution. Rehearsal before opening night.

🔄

Self-Evolving

The orchestra assesses its own gaps and proposes new specialist agents. It doesn't just run — it improves with every performance.
