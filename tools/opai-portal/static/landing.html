Meet Mozart — Your AI Conductor

OPAI reimagines AI orchestration through the language of music. Every task becomes a score. You are the composer. Mozart — the conductor — reads your intent, assembles the right ensemble of specialist agents, and coordinates them through execution. Each agent plays its part, in harmony, on tempo. The result is a performance — delivered as reports, completed code, or decisions ready for your review.

Not pipelines. Not chains. Performances.

Resource-Aware Scheduling

Mozart monitors CPU, memory, and disk in real time. When the system is under load, it defers non-critical work automatically. No dropped notes.

⚖

Human-in-the-Loop

The composer always has final say. Non-trivial decisions surface to you for approval before execution. Rehearsal before opening night.

Self-Evolving System

The orchestra assesses its own gaps and proposes new specialist agents. It doesn't just execute — it improves with every performance.
