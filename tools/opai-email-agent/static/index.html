Welcome to Email Agent

Your AI-powered email assistant that monitors, classifies, and responds to emails on your behalf.

  • 🔎
    Suggestion Mode — AI reads and classifies your emails with tags and priority. No actions taken.
  • ✍
    Internal Mode — AI drafts responses and queues them for your approval before sending.
  • ⚡
    Auto Mode — AI sends responses autonomously (rate-limited). Full control via permissions.
  • 🔒
    Whitelist Only — Only emails from whitelisted senders are processed. Everything else is safely skipped.
  • 🛠
    Granular Controls — Toggle individual permissions (tag, draft, send, move) in the Controls sidebar.
