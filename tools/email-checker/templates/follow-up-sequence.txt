# Template: Follow-Up Sequence (3-Touch)
# Voice: boutabyte-professional
# Trigger: After sending a proposal, quote, or initial outreach with no response

## Subject Line Options
- Re: {{original_subject}}
- Quick follow-up — {{project_name}}
- Checking in — {{project_name}}

## Body — Touch 1 (Day 1 after no response)

Hi {{client_name}},

Just wanted to circle back on the {{document_type}} I sent over {{send_date}}. I know things get busy, so no rush at all — just wanted to make sure it landed in your inbox and see if you had any questions about what we outlined.

If anything needs adjusting or if you'd like to talk through any part of it, I'm happy to set up a quick call whenever works for you. Otherwise, take your time and let me know when you're ready to move forward.

## Body — Touch 2 (Day 3 after no response)

Hi {{client_name}},

Following up on the {{document_type}} for {{project_name}}. Wanted to check if you've had a chance to review it or if there's anything I can clarify.

If the timing isn't right or priorities have shifted, totally understand — just let me know either way so I can plan accordingly.

## Body — Touch 3 (Day 7 after no response)

Hi {{client_name}},

Last check-in on the {{project_name}} {{document_type}}. I want to respect your time, so this will be my final follow-up on this thread.

If you'd like to revisit this down the road, my door is always open. Just reply whenever the timing is better and we'll pick up right where we left off.

## Notes
- Each touch gets progressively shorter and more direct
- Never send more than 3 follow-ups — after Touch 3, move to "cold" status
- Touch 1 is warm and helpful, Touch 2 is concise and practical, Touch 3 gracefully closes the loop
- Space follow-ups: Day 1, Day 3, Day 7 after the original send (not after each follow-up)
- If they reply to any touch, reset the sequence — do not continue automated follow-ups
- Track which touch was last sent via the email agent's action log
